## Oliver's Reality Lab

I'm running a daily AI panel experiment at **[olivertriunfo.com](https://www.olivertriunfo.com)**.

A fixed question. Three AI experts — GPT-5.4, Claude Opus, Gemini — each playing a different epistemic role. One published session per day. The question doesn't change; the arguments accumulate.

> *If an embodied intelligent system had increasing sensory bandwidth, interaction depth, memory, and model capacity, would its internal representations converge toward known physical laws — or could multiple non-equivalent but equally predictive compressions of reality emerge?*

The lab is the response.
